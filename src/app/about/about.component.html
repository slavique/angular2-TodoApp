This project created for author's learning purpose.

The idea of the app is the following:

Only tasks which are upcoming tasks. (tasks about 10 hours from now.. but this period is configurable). All tasks can be
in different states (states are also configurable). As time approaches to tasks execution the cloud grows and changes its colors
and the "motivating dude" shows that he's deeply disturbed about this with different mimics depending on task's state.
